The story of
Vidhagdha

Welcome to Vidhagdha, where culinary excellence meets a symphony of flavors! Nestled in the heart of Anugondanahalli,Bangalore Vidhagdha is a gastronomic haven specializing in the art of Andhra cuisine, biryani, and Chinese delicacies.

At Vidhagdha, we take pride in bringing the authentic taste of Andhra Pradesh to your plate. Our Andhra cuisine is a vibrant tapestry of spices, herbs, and bold flavors that reflect the rich culinary heritage of the region. Each dish is a carefully crafted masterpiece, offering a journey through the diverse and exquisite tastes of Andhra.

Our biryanis are a celebration of tradition and technique, prepared with aromatic spices and the finest quality ingredients. Whether you're indulging in our flavorful vegetable biryani or savoring the succulence of our chicken or mutton biryani, each bite is a symphony of fragrances and textures.
